Welcome to Canvas ‐ Lemmy's r/Place!

We are running a fork of Pxls which modifies authentication and various improvements for running an event.
Similar to r/Place, this is a multiplayer online collaborative canvas that allows you to create anything you can imagine, one pixel at a time. Create amazing works of art together as a team, or solo.

Join in the community either on Lemmy or on Matrix
